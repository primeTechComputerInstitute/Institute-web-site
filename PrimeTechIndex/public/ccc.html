CCC (Course on Computer Concepts)

The Course on Computer Concepts (CCC) is a certification program designed to provide fundamental knowledge about computers and digital literacy. This course is crucial for anyone seeking basic computer proficiency, especially for government jobs and various competitive exams where a basic understanding of computer applications is required.

The curriculum is structured to be accessible to absolute beginners, covering everything from the core components of a computer to using the internet and handling digital financial transactions. By the end of this course, you will be well-equipped with the essential skills to navigate the modern digital world with confidence.

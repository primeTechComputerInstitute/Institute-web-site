ADFA (Advance Diploma in Financial Accounting)

The Advance Diploma in Financial Accounting (ADFA) is a comprehensive program designed to equip students with practical skills in accounting and finance. This course is ideal for commerce students, graduates, and working professionals who want to enhance their financial literacy and career prospects.

The curriculum covers a wide range of essential topics, from foundational bookkeeping principles to modern accounting software and taxation laws. You will gain the knowledge and hands-on experience needed to manage financial records, prepare reports, and handle day-to-day accounting tasks in any organization.

Course Syllabus

Our detailed syllabus ensures you are fully prepared for a career in finance. Key topics include:

  • Fundamentals of Accounting: Principles, concepts, and conventions of financial accounting.
  • Tally Prime: Comprehensive training in Tally for accounting, inventory management, and payroll.
  • GST (Goods and Services Tax): Understanding and applying GST rules for compliance.
  • Income Tax: Introduction to income tax laws and filing procedures.
  • Advanced Excel: Using advanced functions and tools for financial analysis and reporting.
  • Financial Statements: Preparation and analysis of Balance Sheets, Profit & Loss Statements, and Cash Flow Statements.

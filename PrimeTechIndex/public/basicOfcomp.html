What is Basics of Computer?

The "Basics of Computer" course is designed for individuals who are new to technology and want to gain essential computer skills. This program provides a fundamental understanding of computer components, software, and everyday digital tasks. It's the perfect starting point for anyone looking to build confidence and proficiency in using a computer for personal and professional tasks.

Course Syllabus

Our comprehensive syllabus ensures you cover all the core concepts of computer literacy. You will learn practical skills that are immediately applicable in the real world.

  • Introduction to Computers: History of computers, types of computers, and basic terminology.
  • Hardware and Software: Understanding the difference between hardware (e.g., CPU, keyboard, monitor) and software (e.g., operating systems, applications).
  • Operating System Basics: Navigating a Windows or macOS environment, file management, and system settings.
  • Microsoft Office Suite: Hands-on training in essential applications like MS Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Internet and Email: Using web browsers, conducting effective searches, and sending professional emails.
  • Digital Security: Learning about viruses, malware, and safe browsing practices to protect your data.
